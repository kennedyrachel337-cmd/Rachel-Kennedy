How It Works

Our Partnership Process

A straightforward, transparent process designed to get your book in front of readers who will love it.

1

Submit Your Book

Send us your book details via email. Include your title, genre, a brief pitch, and why you believe readers will love it. We review every submission personally.

2

Quality Review

We read your book cover to cover, evaluating writing quality, professional production, and reader impact. Response within 5-7 business days.

3

Partnership Setup

If accepted, we create a premium sales page for your book, handle all marketing, and set up seamless delivery systems. You provide the book—we do the rest.

Grow Together
